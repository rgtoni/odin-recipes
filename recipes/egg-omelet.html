Omelet

Lets get to cracking!

Ready to crack open a few eggs? Great! This is the recipe to making an egg omelet.

Here you will learn how to easily make a fluffy and delcicious omelet that will make anyone's mouth water! Below are the ingredients and steps.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon oil or 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a small bowl and whisk.
  2. Add some salt and pepper, if you like, but do not add any water, milk, or any other liquids.
  3. Heat the oil or butter in a 9-inch non-stick frying pan and pour in the eggs.
  4. In the first 30-seconds of cooking, use a spatula to create 6-10 small cuts through the omelette.
  5. When the top is nearly set, sprinkle any fillings over half of the omelette and turn off the heat.
  6. Use your spatula to flip one half of the omelette over the other and serve immediately.
